HELP & GUIDE

Everything you need to know about using Orion Terminal.

◈ TRADES — LOGGING YOUR TRADES

The TRADES section shows your full trade history. Use the filter tabs to show All, Wins, Losses, Stocks only or Forex only. Click EDIT to modify (1 token) or DEL to delete (1 token).

＋ ADD TRADE — STOCK OR FOREX

Choose STOCK or FOREX tab. For stocks: enter symbol, shares, entry and exit price. For forex: select pair, direction (BUY/SELL), lot size and prices. The live forex price is auto-filled from a live feed. Each trade added costs 1 token. Use the multiplier to add multiple identical trades at once.

⚡ POSITION SIZE CALCULATOR

Enter your account balance, risk percentage, entry price and stop loss. The calculator tells you exactly how many shares or lots to buy so you never risk more than your limit. The R:R calculator shows your Risk-to-Reward ratio.

★ REVIEWS — NOTES & RATINGS

Add detailed notes, a 1–5 star rating and a screenshot to any trade. This is free — no tokens required. Use it to track why you took a trade, what you learned, and whether you followed your rules.

💬 CHAT — TRADING COMMUNITY

A live global chat room for all Orion Terminal users. Share setups, ask questions, discuss the market. Messages refresh automatically every 4 seconds. Keep messages respectful — spam and abuse will result in removal.

📰 NEWS — FOREX ECONOMIC CALENDAR

Live economic calendar showing upcoming news events that move the forex market. Red = High impact (major market mover), Orange = Medium impact, Grey = Low impact. Always check the calendar before entering a trade.
